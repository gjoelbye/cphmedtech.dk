Copenhagen MedTech was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: connect ambitious students with the thriving MedTech industry in Denmark and beyond.

## What We Do

Since our pilot event in April 2022, we've grown to host over 16 events — including our flagship Copenhagen MedTech Innovation Summit, AI in Medicine lectures, hackathons with DTU Skylab and Google Cloud, and consulting case events with McKinsey, Bain, and Accenture.

## Looking Ahead

In 2025, we're expanding internationally with partnerships from MedTech societies in the UK and US. Our annual Innovation Summit on November 18th will be our biggest yet.

Stay tuned for more updates, event recaps, and industry insights right here on our blog.
