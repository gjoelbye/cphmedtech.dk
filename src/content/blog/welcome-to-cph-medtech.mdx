Copenhagen MedTech was founded in 2022 to connect students with the MedTech industry in Denmark.

## What We Do

Since our pilot event in April 2022, we have hosted 17+ events, including our Copenhagen MedTech Innovation Summit, AI in Medicine lectures, hackathons with DTU Skylab and Google Cloud, and consulting case events with McKinsey, Bain, and Accenture.

## Looking Ahead

In 2025, we expanded internationally with partnerships from MedTech societies in the UK and US.

Stay tuned for more updates, event recaps, and industry insights right here on our blog.
